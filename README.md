#### Because you have exactly 120 minutes to complete the exam:   You have an average of 7 minutes per question.

8 questions are typically straightforward "quick wins" (e.g., creating a pod or scaling a deployment) that take 2–3 minutes.
4 questions are moderately involved (e.g., writing a NetworkPolicy or configuring a PVC).
5 questions are complex troubleshooting scenarios (e.g., repairing a broken control plane or upgrading a cluster) that can easily eat up 15+ minutes
